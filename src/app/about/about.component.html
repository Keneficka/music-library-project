About The Amazing Music Library

The Amazing Music Library was originally created to keep a record of the astounding music collection of Lord Abraham Archibald Reginald III. Created by Lord Reginald's descendants at the Abraham Archibald Reginald Music Preservation Foundation to track his unrivaled collection of thousands of Edison Phonograph Cylinders, they have since released this totally awesome music library software to the public.
